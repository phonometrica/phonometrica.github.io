Phonometrica uses (parts of) the following open source components, sometimes with modifications:

  • libsndfile, by Erik de Castro Lopo (LGPL), see www.mega-nerd.com
  • Speex, maintained by Jean-Marc Valin and contributors (BSD license), see www.speex.org
  • RTAudio, by Gary P. Scavone (MIT), see www.music.mcgill.ca/~gary/rtaudio
  • FFTS, by Anthony M. Blake (BSD 3-clause), see github.com/anthonix/ffts
  • SWIPE, by Kyle Gorman (MIT), see github.com/kylebgorman/swipe
  • Snack, by Jonas Beskow and Kåre Sjölander (BSD), see www.speech.kth.se/snack/

  • sendpraat, by Paul Boersma, see www.praat.org

We are also grateful to JetBrains for providing us with an academic license of their C++ editor.

The development of query protocols was developed as part of the following project: A corpus-based longitudinal study of the interphonological features of Japanese learners of French. PI: Sylvain DETEY (Waseda University). This project was supported by the Japanese Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS), Grant-in-Aid for Scientific Research (B) n°23320121 (2011-2014).
